Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

BioQuantix respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: When you register or upgrade, we collect your email address and basic profile information.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous analytics regarding how you interact with our terminal (e.g., assets searched) to improve our machine learning models.
  • Payment Information: We do not store your credit card details. All payments are processed securely by our third-party Merchant of Record (e.g., Paddle / Stripe).

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide, maintain, and improve the BioQuantix terminal, and to process your transactions. We will never sell your personal data or search history to third-party hedge funds or marketing agencies.

3. Cookies

We use essential cookies to keep you logged in and functional cookies to remember your dashboard preferences.
